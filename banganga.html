Banganga

Project Details

**"Banganga"** delves into the spiritual and historical heart of Mumbai, where the tranquil Banganga Tank stands as a testament to the city's layered cultural heritage. This project captures the serene ambiance of this sacred space, set against the vibrant backdrop of modern urban life.

The images explore the daily rituals, timeless architecture, and unique stories that make Banganga a haven for spirituality and community. From the saffron-clad priests performing ceremonies to the reflections on the water mirroring the ancient temples, each photograph tells a tale of continuity and devotion.

This series is an ode to the resilience of tradition amidst the relentless march of progress. It invites viewers to pause, reflect, and immerse themselves in the quiet beauty of this historic oasis in the bustling cityscape.
