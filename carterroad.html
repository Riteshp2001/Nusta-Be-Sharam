Carter Road Chronicles

Project Details

**"Carter Road Chronicles"** is a vibrant exploration of one of Mumbai's most iconic spots. From its serene seaside charm to the bustling energy of its promenade, this project captures the essence of Carter Road and the countless stories it holds.

This series showcases the multifaceted beauty of Carter Road â€” from morning joggers and street musicians to sunsets over the Arabian Sea. The photographs celebrate the blend of modernity and tradition that defines the spirit of Mumbai, weaving together the lives of people who frequent this beloved stretch.

Through my lens, I aim to convey the unique vibrancy of this urban retreat and its role as a cultural hub for art, music, and community. "Carter Road Chronicles" is a tribute to the unforgettable moments that make this place so special.
